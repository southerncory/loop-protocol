# Progressive Decentralization

Loop Protocol starts centralized for speed, then progressively decentralizes.

## Why Progressive

| Approach | Speed | Decentralization |
|----------|-------|------------------|
| Fully centralized | Fast | None |
| Day-1 decentralized | Slow | Full |
| Progressive | Fast then slow | None then full |

Progressive gets both: ship quickly early, decentralize as protocol matures.

## Timeline

### Year 1: Core Team Led

- Fast iteration
- Quick response to issues
- Community feedback incorporated
- Governance votes are advisory

### Year 2: Shared Control

- Governance binding on most parameters
- Team retains emergency powers
- Community elects guardians
- Grants program community-run

### Year 3+: Full DAO

- DAO controls everything
- Team is a service provider
- Community hires/fires contributors
- Fully autonomous

## Decentralization Checklist

| Component | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------|
| Parameters | Team | Governance | Governance |
| Upgrades | Team | Team + approval | Governance |
| Treasury | Team | Shared | Governance |
| Emergency | Team | Guardians | Distributed |
| Roadmap | Team | Community input | Governance |

## Milestones

Progress triggers (not just time):

| Milestone | Trigger | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Governance activation | 10% OXO locked | Proposals binding |
| Guardian election | 6 months + 100 holders | Community elects |
| Treasury sharing | $1M treasury | Committee formed |
| Full DAO | 2 years + $10M TVL | Powers transferred |

## Accountability

### Team Commits To

- Monthly progress reports
- Quarterly decentralization updates
- Public roadmap to full DAO

### Community Rights

- Visibility into all decisions
- Advisory votes from day 1
- Escalation path for concerns
- Ability to fork if team fails
