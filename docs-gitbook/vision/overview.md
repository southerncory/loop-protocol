# Vision Overview

Loop Protocol enables three core activities: **capture** value from everyday activities, **compound** it through stacking and strategies, and **maintain custody** of your own funds.

## Capture

Your agent intercepts value from activities that currently benefit intermediaries:

| Activity | Currently Goes To | With Loop |
|----------|-------------------|-----------|
| Shopping | Banks, card networks | 80-85% to your vault |
| Data | Platforms, brokers | You license on your terms |
| Attention | Advertisers, platforms | Direct payment to you |
| Presence | Location aggregators | You sell proofs directly |

Capture is automatic. You enable modules, continue living normally, and value accumulates in your vault.

## Compound

Captured value grows through multiple mechanisms:

**Stacking**: Lock Cred for yield. Longer locks earn higher rates (3-15% APY). Yield compounds automatically if you configure auto-restacking.

**Agent Strategies**: Your agent can execute wealth-building strategies on your behalf. Reinvesting yields, optimizing stacking durations, dollar-cost averaging into positions.

**Time**: Unlike extracted value that compounds for banks, captured value compounds for you. Small amounts become significant over decades.

## Self-Custody

Loop is infrastructure, not a custodian:

**Your Keys**: You control your wallet. No one else can access your vault without your authorization.

**Your Rules**: You configure how your agent operates. Autonomy levels, spending limits, stacking preferences.

**Your Exit**: You can extract Cred to USDC anytime. A 5% fee applies (to encourage long-term stacking), but you're never locked in.

This isn't a bank account where an institution holds your funds and can freeze them. Your vault is a smart contract you control.

## How It Feels

**Today (without Loop)**:
You buy groceries. The store processes payment. The bank takes fees. Someone sells your data. You have groceries.

**Tomorrow (with Loop)**:
You buy groceries. Your agent verifies the purchase, captures the reward, and routes it to your vault. Your stacking position grows. Over years, these captures compound into significant wealth.

The daily experience is identical. The value flow is opposite.

## Network Growth

The protocol benefits from network effects:

```
More Users
    ↓
More Agent Activity
    ↓
More Merchant Integration
    ↓
More Capture Opportunities
    ↓
More Users
```

As more participants join, capture rates improve, integration expands, and the protocol becomes more valuable for everyone.

## Who Benefits

**Users**: Capture and compound value from daily activities while maintaining full custody.

**Agent Developers**: Integrate Loop SDK to give agents value capture capabilities. Earn fees through Service Agents.

**Merchants**: Lower fees than card networks while accessing engaged, value-conscious customers.

**OXO Stakers**: Share in protocol fees proportional to veOXO position.

## What Loop Is Not

**Not a Bank**: Loop doesn't hold your funds. You do.

**Not an Investment Advisor**: Your agent executes strategies based on your preferences, not Loop's recommendations.

**Not a Get-Rich-Quick Scheme**: Value capture is incremental. Wealth builds over years and decades, not overnight.

**Not a Closed Ecosystem**: Any agent can implement Loop. We don't control which AI powers your agent.
