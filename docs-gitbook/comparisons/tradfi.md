# vs Traditional Finance

How Loop compares to traditional financial systems.

## Core Difference

Traditional finance treats you as a product. Your deposits fund bank lending. Your transactions generate fees. Your data gets sold.

Loop treats you as the beneficiary. Value flows to your vault. You control your funds.

## Comparison

| Dimension | Traditional Finance | Loop Protocol |
|-----------|---------------------|---------------|
| Value flow | To intermediaries | To you |
| Transparency | Opaque | On-chain |
| Fees | Hidden, complex | Clear, minimal |
| Yield | Banks earn (0.01% to you) | You earn (3-15%) |
| Data | Sold without consent | You control |
| Transfers | Days, fees | Seconds, cheap |
| Access | Business hours | 24/7 |
| Custody | Institution holds | You hold |

## Transaction Economics

### $100 Credit Card Purchase

**Traditional**:
- You pay: $100
- Merchant receives: ~$97
- Card network: $0.20
- Issuing bank: $2.30
- You capture: $0

**With Loop**:
- You pay: $100
- Merchant receives: ~$98.50
- Your vault: $1.28
- Protocol: $0.15
- Stakers: $0.07

Over 1,000 transactions: $0 vs $1,280.

## Yield Comparison

On $10,000 balance:

| Institution | APY | Annual Yield |
|-------------|-----|--------------|
| Big bank savings | 0.01% | $1 |
| High-yield savings | 4-5% | $400-500 |
| Loop stacking (6mo) | 8% | $800 |
| Loop stacking (1yr) | 12% | $1,200 |

## Trust Model

**Traditional**: Trust the institution. They can freeze accounts, change terms, sell data.

**Loop**: Trust the code. Open source, on-chain execution, you control your keys.

## Who Wins, Who Loses

**Winners**: Individuals (capture value), small merchants (lower fees), long-term savers.

**Losers**: Card networks, big banks, data brokers.
